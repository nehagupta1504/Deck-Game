Rules of the Game
  1. You will have only 3 minutes to play the game.
  2. There will be 52 playing cards scatterd over & 4 Deck boxes.
  3. You have to place each card in its correct deck box, i.e. spade in spade, heart in heart & so on.
  4. You will loose the game if timer goes off.
  5. If you will complete it within 3 minutes you will win the game
